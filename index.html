Neerja Badhwar (Neena)

(7 April 1952 - 17 October 2025)

A beloved family member and friend; a valued contributor to her community; a newspaper editor; and a lifelong lover of wordplay, culture and language in all its forms.

Please join us to celebrate her life at 2:00 pm on Wednesday, 22 October, at the Lotus Pavilion, Macquarie Park Cemetery & Crematorium, corner Plassey and Delhi Roads, Macquarie Park, NSW 2113.
A livestream will be available on the day: www.neerja.info

A pooja will be held at 2:00 pm on Sunday, 26 October, at her home, 23 Barkala Place, Westleigh, NSW 2120.
