Cormorant-Style Fonts - Heavier Weight Variations

These options are similar to Cormorant Garamond but with more weight/presence. Options 1-4 are variations of Cormorant itself, while 5-8 are similar elegant serif fonts with comparable aesthetics but heavier weights.
